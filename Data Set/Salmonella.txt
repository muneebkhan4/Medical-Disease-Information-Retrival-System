Disease Name: Salmonella
Cause: Salmonella bacteria
Prevalence: Salmonella infection is a common foodborne illness that affects millions of people each year. In the United States, it is estimated that there are 1.2 million cases of Salmonella infection annually.
Symptoms: Diarrhea, fever, and abdominal cramps.
Treatments: Antibiotics and supportive care such as rest and hydration.
Preventive Measures: Proper food handling and preparation, avoiding raw or undercooked eggs and meats, and washing hands frequently.
